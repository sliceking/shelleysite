My Story

I'm on a mission to advance women's healthcare initiatives globally by contributing to early stage femtech start-ups.

I've been where you are—overworked, burned out, and searching for more. With 15 years of experience in both traditional clinical and high-growth startup environments, I've successfully transitioned from bedside nursing to becoming a women's health SME and career consultant.

I'm a registered nurse, certified yoga instructor, and world traveler who speaks four languages: English, Spanish, Hindi, and Punjabi. My Peace Corps experience and work across international teams has given me a global, holistic lens on healthcare.

Having worked at companies like Carrot Fertility, Everly Health, and UnitedHealth Group, I understand how to navigate the femtech and digital health landscape. I know how to take care of people and make things better, which enables a deep understanding of patient and user experience.

Now I help other nurses and clinicians achieve similar transitions into meaningful, well-compensated roles in women's health and digital health companies.
